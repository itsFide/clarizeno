About the Program's Methodologist is Now a Mind Game

This program was developed by Dmitry Gendelman, a specialist in behavioral psychology. His work focuses on understanding how people make decisions and how the habits we build shape our lives.

A key part of his expertise is adapting educational methods to fit different cultural contexts. He believes that for any learning to be effective, it must respect the values and realities of the people it serves.

This training was created with a deep understanding of the Filipino reality. The goal was not to import foreign ideas, but to develop a practical system that resonates with the values of the Filipino family man. He is not a "guru," but a methodologist who has organized practical knowledge into a clear, structured learning system.
